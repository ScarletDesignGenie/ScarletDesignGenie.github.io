Scarlet Design Genie is where
passion + creative “magic”
unite to bring forth the immense power of visual communicative medium as one of our most profound influencers. Tapping into the understanding that we are highly visual beings—our minds are quickly triggered to respond to visual stimuli as forces that influence the way we engage with the world around us; often driving how we think, perceive, and even respond in making decisions.

Becoming a graphic and web designer started as an act of deep curiosity and passion, and has continued to blossom into a lifelong affair marked by fascination and insatiable determination, towards actualizing your full potential, by providing impactful visual communication strategies and design solutions that add immense value to the growth, development and prosperity to your organization.

portfolio

Bill & Melissa Gates Foundation

Press Kit Design

The press kit serves as an essential touchpoint in developing a fundamental understanding of what the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation strives to accomplish, and a call to action to get involved.
The design concept was driven by the need to create a press kit that would position itself as a valued envoy from the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation. An essential part of the design process involved strategically elevating how the press kit is perceived, interacted with, and experienced. Engaging the viewer's tactile sense of touch and tangible reality, would elicit the viewer's innate instinct and drive to form a meaningful connection, engaging its viewer in his/her entirety.
